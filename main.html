My Journey

In my early days, I was just a youngster who was introduced to the wonders of Microsoft Arcade, where I first encountered the mysteries of JavaScript. Fast forward to today, and I find myself honing my coding skills with finesse, mastering C++ and handling C# and C with proficiency, all while excelling in the intricacies of Python. The journey kicked off with the pixelated allure of Microsoft Arcade, where JavaScript first was introduced and caught my attention. As time unfolded, I delved into the complexities of C++, overcoming challenges with determination and grit. Still in the learning phase, I managed to grasp the intricacies of C# and C, honing them into tools at my disposal. During Summer holidays I was introduced to HTML and CSS, expanding my toolkit and allowing me to explore the world of web development. However, the real turning point was Python. Secondary school introduced me to its simplicity and versatility, and it quickly became a language in which I found great proficiency.

Today, I find myself passionate about coding, honing my skills from the days of JavaScript in Microsoft Arcade to mastering the complexities of C++, and becoming adept in C# and C, as well as achieving a high level of proficiency in Python. The flame of passion continues to drive my exploration of the coding universe, eager for whatever challenges lie ahead, whether it be school, home, play or life....
